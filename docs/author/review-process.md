# Content Review Process

## Review Workflow

All content in the Physical AI & Humanoid Robotics Book undergoes a comprehensive review process to ensure quality and accuracy.

### Step 1: Self-Review
Authors perform initial quality checks using the standard checklist:
- [ ] Technical accuracy verified
- [ ] Code examples tested and functional
- [ ] Accessibility standards met
- [ ] Content aligns with learning objectives
- [ ] Proper formatting and structure

### Step 2: Peer Review
Content is reviewed by domain experts:
- Technical accuracy assessment
- Pedagogical effectiveness evaluation
- Consistency with overall curriculum
- Suggestions for improvement

### Step 3: Editorial Review
Final editorial review includes:
- Language and style consistency
- Formatting and structural compliance
- Cross-reference accuracy
- Publication readiness

## Review Timeline

- Initial submission to first review: 5 business days
- Author revision period: 10 business days
- Final review and approval: 3 business days
- Total process: 18 business days average

## Quality Standards

All content must meet the following standards:
- Technical accuracy (verified through testing)
- Pedagogical effectiveness (clear learning progression)
- Accessibility compliance (WCAG 2.1 AA standards)
- Consistency with style guide
